One more day! November 28, 2014

If you didn't know, a few days ago we decided the game Singularity will not be available during competition due to lack of active game developers, but the problems can still be accessed on the problems page once the competition starts. With only one day left till our launch, we have put up a couple sample problems on the About page. And if during competition, you find any bugs, please use the Feedback page to notify us or you can message us privately on the IRC. Also, the end time for the competition has now been updated from 11am Dec 6th to 10pm Dec 6th to allow teams to have more work time (especially on the weekend since most competitors are students and probably more busy during the week).
