The Flatiron School

The Flatiron School runs immersive, full-time adult program to prepare students with little technical experience to build web and mobile applications in just 12 weeks. Our programs have a 7% acceptance rate and 99% of our graduates are now employed as software engineers with an average starting salary of newly $80,000. We have also partnered with The Mayors Office in NYC to bring our program specifically to low-income New Yorkers through the NYC Web Development Fellowship.

Edmodo

Edmodo helps connect all learners with the people and resources they need to reach their full potential.

Educoin

EduCoin (EDU) is an open source peer-to-peer digital currency. EduCoin is money for school, made of bits not debts.

HSCS

HSCS.io empowers passionate computer science high school students to enrich computer science learning in their communities. They will do this by aiding student run events, educating students in CS, and enabling students to run their own events.
